Mama's Spaghetti

Description

You're a child with no responsibilities, and not a care in the world. Mom said dinner's ready, you rush to the table to find her fabled butter noodles. Except you're not a child anymore, you're an adult chasing the nostalgia of a simpler life. Tastes just like home.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water, leaving about an inch and a half from the top
  2. Place pot on stove, and set the dial to high
  3. Leave pot until water starts boiling
  4. Turn dial down to low, and add a generous amount of salt to water
  5. Add spaghetti and stir for roughly 10 minutes
  6. Drain water and noodles into colander, and place noodles on plate
  7. Put small stick of butter in a bowl, and place in microwave for 45 seconds
  8. Pour melted butter on noodles, and sprinkle pepper on top
  9. Voila! Good job, I'm proud of you
