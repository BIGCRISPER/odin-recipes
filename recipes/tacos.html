Prison Tacos

Description

There's beauty in simplicity. Whether you're currently incarcerated in a maximum security prison, or just a broke college student, you can enjoy the magic that is these plain tacos! Just a couple simple ingredients that will change your life forever.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Taco shells
  • Taco seasoning
  • Glass of water

Steps

  1. Place slightly greased pan on stove, turn dial to medium heat
  2. Wait two minutes for pan to heat, then empty raw ground beef onto pan
  3. Slowly break up ground beef using any hard utensil at your disposal
  4. Continue breaking up beef, and stirring to thoroughly cook meat
  5. When meat is fully brown, turn stove to low and add taco seasoning and glass of water
  6. Stir for five minutes until water has evaporated
  7. Throw some taco shells into the microwave for 45 seconds
  8. Remove taco shells, and fill them with your delicious ground beef
  9. Pat yourself on the back for cooking up a five star meal without mom's help
