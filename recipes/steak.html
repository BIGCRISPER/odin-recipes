Exquisite Steak

Description

It's time we put on our big boy pants, and turn on the grill. This five star steak will change your outlook on life. What used to be dull and melancholy, is now full of hope and prosperity.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Love

Steps

  1. Turn your grill to medium-high heat
  2. Throw your steak onto a cutting board and rub salt and pepper on all sides
  3. Caress your steak lovingly, then place it on your grill
  4. Keep the lid closed as we want to trap the heat
  5. Let steak sit for about two and a half minutes before flipping
  6. Continue flipping until inside is about 140 degrees, or basically until it looks done
  7. Carefully remove steak from the grill, and admire your creation
  8. Dig in. You deserve this
